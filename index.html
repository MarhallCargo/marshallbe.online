How we work

A studio with a long view.

These aren't slogans. They're the constraints we hold ourselves to on every release.

Few things, done well

We'd rather ship two products we're proud of than ten we can't stand behind. Focus is the feature.

Privacy by default

We design for data minimisation. Marshall VPN keeps no logs of your activity; Quiet Escape asks almost nothing of you.

Honest pricing

Usable free tiers and simple subscriptions through the App Store and Google Play. No dark patterns, no hidden fees.

Here for the long term

We maintain and improve what we ship. Write to us and you reach the person who actually builds the apps.
