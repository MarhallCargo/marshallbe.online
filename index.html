Marshall Relay Limited is a Hong Kong incorporated company developing privacy-first consumer software. We work in service of one idea — that the people who use technology deserve tools designed in their interest, not against it.

“

Our work is grounded in a simple conviction: good software is, fundamentally, an act of care for the people who use it. We build with privacy as a foundation rather than a feature, and we hold ourselves to standards we'd want any product in our lives to meet — quiet, reliable, and aligned with the user's interests.

Principles

Four ideas that shape how we work.

These aren't marketing values. They're commitments we make to ourselves about the work we ship and the people we ship it for.

01 · Foundation

Privacy as foundation

We engineer for privacy from the first commit. Not as a feature added later for marketing, but as a structural property of how the software is built.

02 · Alignment

User-aligned, always

The people who use our products are not our product. We don't sell their data, profile their behaviour, or treat them as a metric to be optimised against.

03 · Craft

Engineering integrity

We hold ourselves to standards that exceed what's required. We ship work we'd be comfortable defending in any room, and we revisit our own decisions often.

04 · Tone

Quiet excellence

We work without noise. We care about details that aren't immediately visible. We let the product, not the marketing, speak for the company.

Focus

What we work on.

We focus narrowly. Our work centres on consumer software for individual users, distributed through reputable platforms.

01

Privacy-first mobile applications

Native iOS and Android software designed for individual users, with privacy and data minimisation treated as engineering requirements rather than optional features.

02

Connectivity infrastructure

Cloud and network systems that support our applications — sourced from established commercial providers across multiple international regions, operated to a high standard.

03

Subscription products

Honest, simple subscription pricing distributed through Apple App Store and Google Play. No dark patterns, no hidden fees, no advertising, no data resale.

Governance & compliance

Operating responsibly under Hong Kong law.

Annual returns filed with the Hong Kong Companies Registry. Business registration maintained with the Inland Revenue Department.

Tax compliance

Platform compliance

Restricted activities.
